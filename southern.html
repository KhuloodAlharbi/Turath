Rijal Alma (Aseer)

A stunning heritage village known for its colorful stone houses and rich history.

Rijal Alma is a historic village in Asir, famous for its unique architecture and cultural significance. The village has a museum showcasing traditional artifacts and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Jabal Al-Soudah (Abha)

The highest peak in Saudi Arabia, offering scenic views, cool weather, and adventure activities.

Jabal Al-Soudah, standing at over 3,000 meters, is a favorite destination for hiking, camping, and nature enthusiasts. It offers incredible misty landscapes, dense forests, and a refreshing escape from the desert heat.

Wadi Lajab (Jazan)

A beautiful canyon with freshwater streams, waterfalls, and lush greenery.

Wadi Lajab is a hidden gem in the mountains of Jazan, offering a spectacular blend of towering cliffs, running water, and verdant scenery. It is ideal for hiking, swimming, and photography.

Farasan Islands (Jazan)

A paradise for beach lovers, featuring crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and diverse marine life.

Farasan Islands are known for their untouched beaches, excellent diving spots, and rich biodiversity. They offer a perfect getaway for relaxation and water activities, surrounded by stunning natural beauty.
