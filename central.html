Masmak Fortress (Riyadh)

A historic fortress that played a major role in the unification of Saudi Arabia.

Masmak Fortress, built in the 19th century, is one of Riyadh’s most significant landmarks. It was the site of the famous 1902 raid by King Abdulaziz to reclaim Riyadh and unify the country.

Kingdom Tower (Riyadh)

An iconic skyscraper with a breathtaking sky bridge at the top.

Kingdom Tower is one of Riyadh's most recognizable landmarks. It houses luxury hotels, shopping centers, and an observation deck that offers stunning panoramic views of the city.

Historic Diriyah

The first capital of Saudi Arabia and a UNESCO-listed heritage site.

Diriyah is home to the At-Turaif district, a UNESCO World Heritage site that showcases the rich history of the Saudi royal family and traditional Najdi architecture.

Wadi Hanifa

A stunning natural valley perfect for relaxation and outdoor activities.

Wadi Hanifa stretches across Riyadh and is a serene escape filled with green landscapes, walking trails, and small lakes, making it a favorite among nature lovers.
