Khobar Water Tower

Located on an artificial island, this tower provides breathtaking panoramic views of the Arabian Gulf.

The Khobar Water Tower is one of the most iconic structures in the Eastern Province. It features an observation deck and a rotating restaurant offering 360-degree views of the Gulf. At night, the tower is beautifully illuminated, making it a fantastic sight from the shoreline.

King Abdulaziz Center for World Culture (Ithra)

A futuristic cultural hub blending art, science, and history in a spectacular architectural design.

The King Abdulaziz Center for World Culture, known as Ithra, is a landmark that combines art, literature, and technology. It houses a museum, a library, a cinema, and various interactive exhibitions that showcase Saudi Arabia’s rich heritage and innovation.

King Fahd Causeway

Connecting Saudi Arabia and Bahrain, this bridge is a remarkable engineering masterpiece.

Spanning over 25 kilometers, the King Fahd Causeway is a crucial link between Saudi Arabia and Bahrain. The bridge features a scenic island in the middle where visitors can stop, dine, and enjoy the view. It is one of the busiest international crossings in the region.
